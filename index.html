Picture of the month

A for aardvark. I first learnt of this curious creature from an old cartoon back in the day. Found across Africa, they spend their days in dark burrows to avoid the heat. They mainly feed on ants and termites. They emerge from their burrows after sunset and start foraging for ants and termites making sure to not repeat their routes for 5-8 days so that termite mounds can recover. Smart! Old aardvark burrows are used by other animals and birds and this protects them during wildfire seasons. Fascinating creatures!
