Picture of the month

This is a southern three banded armadillo. Despite the name of this site, this is the first time I'm posting an armadillo picture. When choosing the name, I only wanted something that had a nice rhyme and rhythm. There are 21 species of armadillos and they all have a leathery armour shell composed of keratin. The two species in the genus Tolypeutes are the only ones that curl up into a ball when threatened (the others can't because they have too many plates!). Native to the Americas, armadillos are threatened by habitat loss and climate change.
