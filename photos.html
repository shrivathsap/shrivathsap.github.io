These are photos I took. An (R) indicates that the photos was taken on my old mobile phone (Redmi 4), an (S) indicates my Samsung M32/M12/A15, a (D) indicates that it was taken on a Nikon 3500 DSLR camera, and (E) indicates that the photo was edited. At the present time I do not know how to properly edit photos, so when I say edited, it refers to the options that my phone has : brightness, sharpness and contrast; and these apply only to the photos taken on my phone. They have been cropped and compressed so as to reduce their file size.
